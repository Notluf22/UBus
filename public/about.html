About UBus

UBus is a smart, real-time ticket booking and location tracking platform designed specifically for short-distance government buses. Inspired by transit helpers like Where is My Train, UBus digitizes the local commute experience through real-time GPS tracking and instant paperless tickets.

Project Overview

UBus empowers local commuters by offering features previously reserved only for luxury or long-distance coaches:

  • General, unreserved mobile ticket booking for local routes.
  • Live tracking of active buses via hardware GPS integration.
  • Highly accurate Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) predictions at each stop.
  • Seamless contactless boarding with digital passes.

Bridging the Digital Divide

Public transport networks in rural and semi-urban hubs face substantial accessibility hurdles due to a lack of live updates and rigid, paper-based cash transactions. UBus provides an accessible, high-tech, and lightweight interface designed to operate seamlessly on any device.

Development Journey

UBus was developed as a visual and functional prototype during an intensive internship program, aimed at solving real-world transit accessibility issues through modern cloud infrastructure (Firebase & Supabase).
