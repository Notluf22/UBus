UBus – Your Route, Your Time

UBus is an innovative ticket booking and real-time tracking system designed for short-distance government buses. Inspired by solutions like Where is My Train, UBus digitizes the local commute experience with smart booking and live location tracking.

Project Overview

UBus empowers commuters by offering:

  • Online booking for general, short-distance bus tickets
  • Live tracking of buses via GPS
  • Accurate Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) at stops
  • Contactless travel with digital passes

Challenges in Traditional Transport

Rural and semi-urban transport systems often lack:

  • Real-time bus arrival information
  • Online booking or payment options
  • Reliable schedule visibility

Our Solution

UBus addresses these gaps with a modern web-based platform that allows users to:

  • Search and view buses by route and availability
  • Book general tickets using any device
  • Track buses in real-time through GPS
  • Make secure digital payments

Live GPS Tracking

Using external GPS modules or built-in emergency systems on buses, UBus provides accurate location data to minimize wait times and enhance commuter planning.

Future Enhancements

  • Offline ticketing support in low-network zones
  • AI-powered passenger crowd prediction
  • Multi-language support for local accessibility
  • Dedicated mobile apps for Android and iOS

Development

This project was built during a 2-week internship CRUD program by a team of 4, aimed at improving the daily commute for underserved regions.
