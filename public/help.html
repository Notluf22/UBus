Help Center

Find answers to common questions about ticket booking, GPS tracking, and boarding passes.

Once you are logged in, simply navigate to the Dashboard. Enter your starting location and destination to view available buses operating on that route. Choose your preferred bus, select your seat, enter your passenger details, and hit confirm. Your digital boarding pass will be created instantly!

Yes. Government buses are integrated with active GPS hardware modules (or panic panic-button nodes). These coordinates are broadcast directly to our Firestore database in real-time, giving you accurate ETAs at each bus stop so you never miss your ride.

No paper ticket is needed! UBus provides a digital pass directly on your smartphone screen. Simply show the pass to the conductor. The high-contrast E-Ticket displays a QR code that contains your active booking details for verification.

We support digital ticketing payments including secure UPI gateways, digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe), credit cards, and debit cards. The booking is instantly confirmed once the payment is made successfully.
